About Us

WHO WE ARE?

At WildGuardians, we are committed to the preservation and protection of endangered species worldwide. Our mission is to create awareness, provide resources for conservation efforts, and take direct action to save vulnerable wildlife from extinction. With over a decade of experience, our team of environmentalists, biologists, and volunteers works tirelessly to protect Earth’s most precious creatures and their habitats.

Projects

WHAT WE DID?

  • Project Gorilla Guardians – A dedicated effort to protect mountain gorillas in Rwanda’s Volcanoes National Park from poaching and habitat destruction.
  • Project Elephant Revival – Supporting the reintroduction and protection of elephants in Rwanda’s Akagera National Park through anti-poaching efforts.
  • Project Akagera Shield – A holistic effort to conserve Rwanda’s Akagera National Park, protecting lions, rhinos, leopards, and other species.
  • Project Savannah Shield – A dedicated effort to protect the last remaining herds of African gorillas from poaching in Rwanda’s savannah.
