Learn More About WildHaven

WHAT WE DO

At WildHaven, we focus on protecting endangered species like mountain gorillas and elephants in Rwanda and across Africa. We run anti-poaching patrols, restore habitats, educate communities, and promote sustainable ecotourism to ensure wildlife thrives.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to create a world where wildlife and humans coexist sustainably. We aim to protect endangered species, preserve their habitats, and empower local communities through education and economic opportunities.

OUR VISION

We envision a future where Africa’s wildlife, from gorillas in Rwanda to elephants in Kenya, flourishes in safe, thriving ecosystems, supported by global and local communities united in conservation efforts.
