Our Projects

WHAT WE’RE DOING

Project Gorilla Guardians

Protecting mountain gorillas in Rwanda’s Volcanoes National Park from poaching and habitat loss.

>

Project Elephant Revival

Supporting the reintroduction and protection of elephants in Rwanda’s Akagera National Park.

>

Project Akagera Shield

Conserving Rwanda’s Akagera National Park, protecting lions, rhinos, and leopards.

>

Community Education Program

Educating communities near Rwanda’s parks to reduce human-wildlife conflict.

>

Anti-Poaching Patrols

Deploying patrols to combat poaching across Rwanda’s national parks.

>

Habitat Restoration Initiative

Restoring degraded habitats in Rwanda to support wildlife populations.
